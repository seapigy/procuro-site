Frequently Asked Questions

Short answers to the most common activation and trust questions.

How long does setup take?

Most teams finish QuickBooks connection in under a minute and can review initial data shortly after activation.

Do I need a credit card to start?

No. Your 14-day trial starts without a card, so your team can validate value before committing.

What QuickBooks data does Procuro use today?

Current functionality is purchase-derived: Procuro reads purchase transactions to infer item and vendor hints. It does not run a separate direct Item or Vendor catalog sync endpoint today.

What data does Procuro not access?

Procuro does not use customer lists, invoices, payroll, or banking data for this savings workflow.

Can we cancel anytime?

Yes. You can cancel at any time from settings. Your access remains active through the paid period if applicable.

Where can I get help?

Use Support or email procuroapp@gmail.com. Typical first response is within one business day.
