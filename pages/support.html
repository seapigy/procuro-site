Common Questions

  • Connection issues: If QuickBooks data isn't importing, click "Reconnect QuickBooks" on your dashboard. Ensure you've granted all required permissions during the OAuth flow.
  • Price data: Procuro checks trusted retailer APIs daily (Amazon, Walmart, Target) to ensure accuracy. Prices are updated automatically, and you'll receive alerts when better deals are found.
  • Security: We never store your QuickBooks credentials or financial data. We only access purchase history and vendor information needed to find savings opportunities.
  • Disconnecting: You can disconnect Procuro from your QuickBooks account at any time via the QuickBooks Apps settings page or by contacting our support team.
  • Savings calculations: Estimated savings are based on your purchase frequency and current retail prices. Actual savings may vary based on shipping costs, taxes, and availability.

Technical Support

If you're experiencing technical issues, please include the following information when contacting support:

  • Your QuickBooks company name
  • Browser and version
  • Description of the issue
  • Any error messages you're seeing
