Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Procuro values your privacy. We access limited QuickBooks data solely to analyze purchasing activity and identify potential savings opportunities. We do not sell, rent, or share your business information with third parties.

Information We Collect

Data We Access from QuickBooks:

  • Vendor names and contact information
  • Item descriptions and product names
  • Purchase quantities and frequencies
  • Prices paid for goods and services
  • Purchase dates and transaction history

Data We Never Access:

  • Customer records or customer contact information
  • Payroll or employee information
  • Tax records or filings
  • Bank account or financial account details
  • Credit card numbers or payment methods

How We Use Your Information

We use the data accessed from your QuickBooks account to:

  • Identify frequently purchased items and supplies
  • Compare your purchase prices with current retail prices
  • Generate savings alerts when better deals are available
  • Calculate potential savings based on your purchase frequency
  • Provide personalized recommendations for cost optimization

Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. All data transmission is protected using HTTPS/TLS 1.3 encryption. Data at rest is stored in encrypted databases with industry-standard security measures.

We use OAuth 2.0 for QuickBooks authentication, which means:

  • We never see or store your QuickBooks password
  • Access tokens are encrypted in our database
  • You can revoke access at any time through QuickBooks

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your business data with third parties.

We only share data with:

  • Retailer APIs: We send item search queries (product names only) to Amazon, Walmart, and Target APIs to find current prices. We do not share your purchase history or company information.
  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting providers (for secure data storage) and email services (for alert notifications) under strict data protection agreements.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we store about you
  • Correction: Request corrections to any inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Disconnect: Revoke Procuro's access to your QuickBooks account at any time

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your QuickBooks connection is active. If you disconnect Procuro from your QuickBooks account, we will delete your data within 30 days, unless required to retain it for legal or regulatory purposes.

Cookies and Tracking

Procuro uses minimal cookies for:

  • Session management and authentication
  • User preferences (theme, dashboard settings)

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking scripts.

Children's Privacy

Procuro is a business-to-business (B2B) application intended for commercial use only. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

For any privacy-related inquiries or to exercise your data rights, please contact: privacy@procuroapp.com
